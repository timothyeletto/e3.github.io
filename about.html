About E3 Productions


E3 Productions is a film productions company with an extremely low budget. This company runs off of pocket money which is why the only cast and crew that we have is three siblings and a friend. Also, almost all of us have multiple jobs. We're still trying to get our company up and running so if you want to collaborate or know someone who wants to collaborate, definitely contact us. Any sort of help would be great. We also want to grow the filmmaking community so spread the word to try to get all of the low budget filmmakers to team up and make a whole community of filmmakers.
