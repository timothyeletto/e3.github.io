Timothy Eletto
Timothy is an actor and pretty much everything else for E3 Productions. He was the one that started the YouTube channel and the entire company. He is an Actor, Writer, Editor, Producer, and Director. You may be thinking, "If Timothy does all of that, why doesn't anyone else help out and take some of those jobs?" The reason that no one else takes any of Timothy's jobs is because Timothy actually likes having all of these jobs because he likes having a lot of control so he won't give anyone else and of his jobs.

Gregory Eletto
Gregory is an actor and Graphic Designer for E3 Productions. This means that he acts in the skits, and he makes any pictures that are needed such as thumbnails and the picture on the about page. He uses Photoshop CS6 to cut out pictures of the people in the skit and put them onto one picture that looks good and grabs your eye. He is also very often relied on to find people that we can use in skits and other productions since he is a lot more social than Timothy.

Rosie Eletto
Rosie is an actor and 2nd AC. This means that she acts in the skits, and claps the production slate which is used to line up the video and external audio while editing. She is also a grip which means that she basically does anything that Timothy tells her to do and gets anything that Timothy may need. The reason that this is so surprising is because she's only 10 and she's the youngest out of all the cast and crew. However, she actually volunteered to be the grip. She would also have to get people involved with E3 Productions, like Gregory does, but the only people that she knows are little kids and we can't make little kids work for us without pay.
